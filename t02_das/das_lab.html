Direct-Attached Storage Problems

  1. A disk has a full-stroke seek time of 39 ms. Calculate its average seek time.

  2. A disk has average seek time 11 ms. Calculate its full-stroke seek time.

  3. Determine the average rotational latency for a 7200 RPM drive.

  4. A disk has seek time of 10 ms, average rotational latency of 5 ms and transfer time of 2 ms. Calculate the average disk service time.

  5. Calculate the number of IOPS a drive can perform if it has a disk service time of 15 ms.

  6. A disk can perform 83 IOPS. Determine its disk service time.

  7. A 7200 RPM drive has a full-stroke seek time of 18 ms, transfers data internally at a rate of 40 MB/s in blocks of 32 kB. Determine the IOPS.

  8. Disk service time is 18 ms at controller utilisation 50%. Calculate expected disk service time at 70% utilisation.

  9. A drive is capable of performing 160 IOPS. Calculate the maximum permissable IOPS if the utilisation is limited to 60%.

  10. An application requires 2950 IOPS. Available disks can provide a maximum of 160 IOPS. Determine how many disks are required if the utilisation is to be limited to 60%.
